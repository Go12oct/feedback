Common questions

Will my teacher know I used AI?

No. This is a practice tool, not a cheating tool. You upload your practice answers, we mark them like a teacher would, you learn from the feedback and improve. It's the same as getting feedback from a tutor - completely allowed.

How accurate is the AI marking?

We tested against 50 real examiner-marked answers. Our AI matched the examiner's marks 89% of the time, and was within ±1 mark 96% of the time. We're constantly improving by comparing with real mark schemes.

Can it read my handwriting?

Yes. We use OCR technology that works with most handwriting. Just make sure your photo is clear, well-lit, and text is in focus. If it can't read something, it'll tell you to re-upload.

What if I disagree with the marking?

You can flag any marking you think is wrong. We review all flagged responses with a real economics teacher and use that feedback to improve the AI. Your feedback makes the system better for everyone.

Does it work for all Edexcel questions?

Currently works for most A-Level Edexcel Economics questions (Paper 1, 2, and 3). We're adding more question types every week. If your question type isn't supported yet, we'll let you know and add it to our roadmap.
